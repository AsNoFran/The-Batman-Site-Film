Here are the TV series that I recommend related to the dark knight.

Gotham

How not to mention Batman without talking about his childhood in the city he will have to protect in the future? It's impossible. If you want to know the origins of Bruce Wayne then this is the right series. Don't expect to see the batman costume but on the other hand you will see the future villains who will arrive later in the city of Gotham.

Titans

Dick Grayson, Batman's protege, has become an adult and has definitively emancipated himself from his mentor. He left Gotham City to work for the Detroit police. He then meets the young Rachel Roth, an empath, with powers that she can't really master, who interests a strange sect. Helped by Starfire and Beast Boy, he will then form a group of superheroes to protect the young girl.
