Robert Pattinson as Batman

Robert Pattinson was born on 13 May 1986 in London. He is an English actor. Starring in both big-budget and independent films, Pattinson has been ranked among the world's highest-paid actors.

His best known films are Harry Potter and the Goblet of fire, The Twilight Saga, Remeber Me, The Lost City of Z, Tenet and will star as the DC Comics superhero Batman in Matt Reeves's film The Batman.
